April Savings Account

The April Savings Account is a smart investment product that allows users to earn daily interest on their cryptocurrency deposits. The principal deposits are in SOL and can be withdrawn at any time. Daily interest payments are made in AprilCoin, DogCoin, and SkiCoin with the interest rate equivalent to the APR divided by 365. Interest is also paid on the DogCoin, AprilCoin, and SkiCoin daily balances in the savings account. The initial SOL deposit amount is guaranteed and can be reclaimed at any time.
