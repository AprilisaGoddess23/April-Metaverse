Cryptocurrencies

AprilCoin

AprilCoin is an SPL token built on Solana. It will serve as the official currency of the April Metaverse once the metaverse is built. It will also be used as an airdrop reward for future holders of April Metaverse digital land NFTs. Additionally, it will be used as a reward for staking AprilCoin once staking is introduced. Lastly, it will serve as a governance token for voting on changes to the April Metaverse.

Tokenomics:

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000
Max Supply: 1,000,000,000
Supply is capped at 1,000,000,000 coins

Token Distribution:
  • 50% to SOL/APRIL liquidity pool on Raydium. Original coin price is set at $139 in SOL for 500,000,000 coins.
  • 20% to airdrops for Trillionaire NFT and digital land NFTholders.
  • 10% for staking rewards.
  • 10% to Marketing
  • 10% to Founder

NFT Collections

Trillionaire NFTs

The Trillionaire NFTs are the official profile picture NFTs for the April Investments ecosystem. These NFTs will give holders airdrop rights to AprilCoin, Trillionaire Land digital land NFTs, Trillionaire Real Estate NFTs, and Trillionaire Metaverse currency.

The Trillionaire NFTs will be tradable and sellable as individual NFTs on the Solana blockchain.

Trillionaire Digital Land NFTs

Trillionaire Digital Land NFTs will be digital land on the Solana blockchain that will be airdropped to holders of Trillionaire NFTs. The land can be sold and traded as individual NFTs.

Trillionaire Real Estate NFTs

Trillionaire Real Estate NFTs will be airdropped to holders of Trillionaire Digital Land NFTs. The real estate can be sold and traded as individual NFTs.
