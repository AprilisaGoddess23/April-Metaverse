DogCoin (DOG)

DogCoin is an SPL token built on the Solana blockchain and serves as a global currency and investment vehicle.

Tokenomics:
  • Total Supply: 27,000,000,000,000
  • Max Supply: 27,000,000,000,000
  • Supply is capped at 27,000,000,000,000 coins
Token Distribution:
  • 70% to original SOL/DOG liquidity pool on Raydium. Original coin price was set at 0.72 SOL for 18,900,000,000,000 coins. Original LP tokens were burned to prevent future minting.
  • 10% to a locked, LP-funded DogCoin reserve controlled by April Investments.
  • 20% to the founders, with 5% going to a locked reserve, 7% to various angel investors, and 8% to the core team.
