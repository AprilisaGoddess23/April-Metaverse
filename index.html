Trillionaire Dogs and Trillionaire Cats are collections of dogs and cats on the Moon. 72% APR on original USD purchase price projected for holders. APR is based on NFT trade volume revenue (1% of trade volume) and liquidity mining profits. Monthly payments are made in DogCoin or CatCoin, depending on which NFT type was purchased. Each NFT collection will have a capped supply of 1,000,000,000. NFTs will be available to buy and sell fractionally.

Solid Gold Token (SGT)

127% APR on original USD purchase price projected for holders. APR is based on a higher percentage of NFT trade volume revenue (2% of trade volume) and liquidity mining profits. Payments are made in DogCoin, CatCoin, SkiCoin, AprilCoin, or a mix of them. Minimum investment is $25,000.
